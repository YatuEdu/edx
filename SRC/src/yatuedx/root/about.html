About Us

Coding can be boring and difficult if taught by inexperienced “experts”. As Albert Einstein said: “The definition of genius is taking the complex and making it simple”. And the “genius” in this case are true experts who have real world experience in software product building. We are former Microsoft Developer Leads, who have combined experience of coding for over sixty years. The chief instructor has worked on Windows Storage and Security at MS for 15 years and moved on to work at big consulting positions ranging from Shenzhen Stock Exchange and Disney Streaming after leaving Microsoft. He has recently written an advanced C++ book ( The Book) and has been devoted to coding education and consulting. He and another former Microsoft Dev Lead co-founded CodingKingAcademy.
