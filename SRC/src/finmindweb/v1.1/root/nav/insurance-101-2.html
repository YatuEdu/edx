What is life insurance?

Life insurance products are designed to PROTECT PEOPLE FINANCIALLY, and they do this by guaranteeing a specific amount of money to replace the financial loss suffered by a family as a result of a family member:

  • Dying too soon
  • Living too long; or
  • Becoming disabled from sickness or accident

A family’s daily life can drastically change for the worse, and its financial position can deteriorate very quickly when such events occur. With the benefits that life insurance provides, a family can weather severe financial storms and continue living in security and with peace of mind.
